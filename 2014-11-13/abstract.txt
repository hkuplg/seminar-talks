Title: f2ji -- REPL for f2j

Speaker: Emma

Data & Time: Thursday,13 November, 2014 1:45 pm

Venue: CB 313

Abstract:

I'll first briefly introduce how the REPL is built. Then I'll
introduce and present current features of the REPL, and then briefly
talk about how some of its infrastructure can be conveniently used to
potentially enhance the performance of program testing
